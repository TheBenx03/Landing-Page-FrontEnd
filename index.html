Control Team

The Control Team monitors the employees and Abnormalities and plans the best course of action. They give immediate orders to other employees while watching the CCTV feeds.

Information Team

The Information Team analyzes the Abnormalities, profiles them, and devises solutions to issues the Abnormalities may cause based on the data. They are in charge of collecting, analyzing, and archiving observation data and interview logs provided by the Welfare Team.

Safety Team

The Safety Team gives safety training to new employees and develops action plans for all kinds of potential emergencies that can occur in the company.
